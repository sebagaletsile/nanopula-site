Our Solutions

Nanopula's innovative approaches to carbon capture and climate restoration

Carbon Capture-as-a-Service (CCaaS)

Nanopula designs, installs, and operates modular carbon capture systems tailored to industrial clients. Using our nanomaterial technology, we deliver verified CO₂ removal as a measurable service, not just a product sale.

On-site CO₂ monitoring and data tracking

Operations and maintenance by Nanopula technicians

Integration with carbon accounting and credit frameworks

Ocean Alkalinity Enhancement-as-a-Service

Nanopula partners with coastal operators and climate funds to deploy mineral-dosed aerogels that enhance ocean alkalinity and support durable ocean-based carbon removal. We manage deployment, monitoring, and impact reporting as a full service.

Site assessment and alkalinity baseline studies

Deployment of mineral-dosed aerogels in targeted coastal zones

Continuous monitoring of alkalinity and carbon removal performance

MRV-ready data streams to support blue carbon and impact financing

Regenerative Mining Service

Nanopula works with mining operators to transform tailings and waste streams into climate and resource assets. Our nanomaterial systems are designed to capture CO₂, immobilize harmful ions, and recover valuable metals where feasible.

Characterisation of tailings chemistry and CO₂ mineralization potential

Deployment of aerogel-based systems for maximised pure metal recovery.

Stabilisation of hazardous ions to reduce environmental risk

Integrated reporting for ESG, carbon accounting, and regulatory compliance

Nanopula's Solution

Affordable & Modular

Nanopula’s nanocellulose aerogel systems offer low-energy CO₂ capture at affordable cost. Designed for African industrial realities, they are modular, scalable, and efficient.

CCaaS Business Model

Instead of selling materials, Nanopula installs and operates the systems—clients only pay per ton of verified CO₂ captured. Zero upfront cost, full decarbonization impact.
